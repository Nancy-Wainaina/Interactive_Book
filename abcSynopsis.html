ABC- Anti Bathing Club

The ABC is a comic story by Gum Zach. It looks into the life of three naughty friends,the ABC gang; Alberto,Collins and Betty, who hail from Thio City.
The ABC gang loves making trouble and hates being clean.
Led by Betty, they avoid soap and water at all costs. But when the SPD, a group that loves cleanliness, catches them, a wild fight breaks out.
During the chaos, both groups learn surprising things about each other.
Betty also has a confrontantion with a boy named Wilz, which causes more problems. Will the SPD manage to clean up Thomio City and get rid of the ABC gang?
Join the fun to find out!
