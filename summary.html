Unlucky

"Unlucky" follows the journey of Ella, who has always felt cursed by misfortune. Despite the villagers' whispers and her own doubts, she discovers inner strength through the guidance of a mysterious stranger, Mr. Thorne. With his help, Ella learns emotional resilience and uncovers a prophecy that she is destined to save her village from great peril. When a sorcerer threatens their home with a thick fog, Ella leads the villagers in a courageous battle, defeating the sorcerer and restoring peace. Through her journey, Ella transforms from a symbol of bad luck to a beacon of hope and resilience, leaving a legacy of courage and inspiration for generations to come.
