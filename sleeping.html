Sleeping Beauty

"Sleeping Beauty" is a timeless fairy tale set in a kingdom where Princess Aurora is cursed by the malevolent sorceress Maleficent to fall into a deep sleep on her sixteenth birthday, only to be awakened by true love's kiss. To protect her, she is raised in seclusion by three good fairies. Despite their efforts, Aurora pricks her finger on a spindle and falls into a deep slumber, along with the entire kingdom. Prince Phillip, who has fallen in love with Aurora, battles Maleficent in her dragon form to break the curse. With true love's kiss, Aurora awakens, and the kingdom is freed from the curse. Aurora and Phillip marry, ruling the kingdom together, and their love story becomes a legend of hope and bravery, ensuring a happily ever after for all.
